# BRIFTECH LANDING PAGE - DESIGN BRIEF

## 1. Tong quan du an

### Thuong hieu

- Ten thuong hieu: **BrifTech**
- Mo hinh: Cong ty cung cap dich vu phat trien website va san pham so
- Logo: Chua co logo chinh thuc, tam thoi su dung wordmark `BrifTech`
- Thi truong: Viet Nam, phuc vu khach hang tren toan quoc
- Ngon ngu: Giao dien co bo chuyen `VI / EN`; phien ban dau chi can hoan thien noi dung tieng Viet
- Chuyen doi chinh: Khach hang nhan tin qua **Zalo**

### Muc tieu landing page

1. Gioi thieu nang luc phat trien website va he thong quan tri cua BrifTech.
2. Dinh vi BrifTech la doi tac phat trien san pham so toan dien, khong chi la don vi lam website theo mau.
3. Tao cam giac cong nghe cao, cao cap, chuyen nghiep va dang tin cay.
4. Giup khach hang nhanh chong hieu dich vu, quy trinh, chi phi va chinh sach.
5. Thuc day khach hang nhan Zalo de duoc tu van va nhan bao gia.

## 2. Doi tuong khach hang

- Doanh nghiep nho va vua

## 3. Dinh vi thuong hieu

### Dinh vi cot loi

**BrifTech la doi tac phat trien san pham so toan dien, tu phan tich nghiep vu, thiet ke den xay dung, ban giao va dong hanh sau trien khai.**

Website doanh nghiep la dich vu mui nhon, nhung giao dien can the hien duoc nang luc trien khai ca website ban hang, landing page va he thong quan tri lon.

### Khac biet can truyen tai

- Chi phi hop ly, minh bach
- Toc do trien khai nhanh
- UI/UX thiet ke rieng theo tung thuong hieu
- Co BA phan tich bai toan va nghiep vu truoc khi phat trien
- Khach hang lam viec va trao doi truc tiep trong suot du an
- Co kha nang phat trien tu website don gian den CRM, HRM, ERP tuy chinh
- Bao hanh, bao tri va dong hanh sau ban giao

### Tinh cach thuong hieu

- Cong nghe cao
- Sac net va tien phong
- Cao cap, toi gian
- Dang tin cay
- Nhanh nhung khong voi vang
- Sang tao nhung van tap trung vao hieu qua kinh doanh

1. **Tu van:** Tim hieu muc tieu, nhu cau va ngan sach.
2. **Phan tich BA:** Lam ro nghiep vu, nguoi dung, tinh nang va pham vi.
3. **Thiet ke UI/UX:** Xay dung cau truc, wireframe va giao dien theo thuong hieu.

6. **Ban giao va dong hanh:** Trien khai, huong dan, bao hanh va ho tro sau ra mat.

Quy trinh nen duoc trinh bay nhu mot dong chay lien tuc, cho thay khach hang luon biet du an dang o dau.

## 10. Art direction

Tai lieu tham chieu chi tiet: `Landing_DESIGN.md`.

### Huong hinh anh

- Dark-first voi nen `#121316` va `#191A1D`
- Purple `#A77EFF` la mau thuong hieu va CTA chinh
- Blue `#2589FF` dung cho trang thai phu va lien ket
- Magenta `#FF7DBB` dung tiet che lam diem nhan
- Be mat toi, vien inset mo va glass effect nhe
- Hinh anh portfolio va 3D la diem nhan thi giac, khong dung decoration vo nghia
- Chuyen dong muot, tinh te, phuc vu phan cap noi dung

### Cam giac mong muon

Giao dien phai tao cam giac day la mot cong ty co nang luc xay dung san pham cong nghe thuc su: hien dai, chinh xac va co chieu sau. Khong thiet ke theo phong cach template agency pho thong.

### Typography

- Uu tien system UI hoac mot sans-serif hien dai ho tro tieng Viet tot
- Headline hero can manh va co khoang tho
- Body text phai du lon de doc tot; khong ap dung co chu 11px cua trang tham chieu cho noi dung marketing dai
- Chi dung weight 400-600 de giu cam giac tinh gon
- Letter spacing bang 0

### UI principles

- Nut CTA chinh dang pill theo design system
- Card toi da 8px radius cho cac khoi chuc nang; portfolio visual co the dung radius lon hon neu can
- Khong long card trong card
- Khong bien moi section thanh mot floating card
- Icon su dung bo icon dong nhat
- Hieu ung hover ro rang nhung khong gay nhieu
- Dam bao tuong phan va kha nang tiep can

## 11. Hero 3D Spline

### Scene tam thoi

`https://my.spline.design/boxeshover-tNGHXkOweLOVADrVKsIYFUkZ/`

### Cach su dung

- Scene la visual full-bleed hoac tich hop truc tiep vao khong gian hero, khong dat trong mot khung preview trang tri.
- Text va CTA phai luon doc ro, khong bi 3D che lap.
- Desktop cho phep tuong tac hover/cursor.
- Mobile can co bo cuc va camera rieng; neu hieu nang khong dat thi dung anh fallback.
- Hien loading state nhe trong khi scene tai.
- Ton trong `prefers-reduced-motion`.
- Kiem tra hieu nang thuc te truoc khi chot production.

Neu co quyen truy cap scene, uu tien export bang Spline Viewer hoac `scene.splinecode` de kiem soat responsive va interaction tot hon. Public URL hien tai van co the dung lam iframe trong prototype.

## 12. Responsive

### Desktop

- Hero rong, 3D la tin hieu thi giac dau tien
- Noi dung theo container toi da 1280-1440px
- Bang gia co the so sanh truc tiep bon cot

### Tablet

- Dich vu va portfolio chuyen thanh hai cot
- Bang gia chuyen thanh carousel hoac card grid hai cot
- Giam cuong do va chi phi render cua 3D neu can

### Mobile

- Uu tien headline, CTA va bang gia de doc
- Menu thu gon
- Cac khoi noi dung mot cot
- Khong de 3D chiem het man hinh hoac can tro cuon
- CTA Zalo co the hien thi dang sticky o vung thuan tien
- Tat ca touch target toi thieu 44px

## 13. Micro-interaction

- Header thay doi be mat nhe khi cuon
- Section reveal nhe theo opacity va translate
- Card portfolio co preview motion hoac image shift nhe
- Pricing hover lam ro cot dang duoc xem
- Process co progress line hoac highlight theo vi tri cuon
- CTA Zalo co feedback ro rang khi hover/tap
- Khong lam dung parallax, glow, blur hoac animation lap vo tan
